Email us to reserve a space

What is Code Club?

Code Club is about giving kids an opportunity to learn to code, no matter who they are or where they come from.

Our volunteers guide your kids through a range of step-by-step projects, in Scratch, HTML/CSS, and Python.

Everyone is free to learn at their own pace – and, above all, to have a bit of fun!
