What do kids say about Liverpool Code Club?

We believe all children should have the opportunity to learn to code, no matter who they are or where they come from.

That’s why we’ve been introducing Scratch, HTML, and Python to kids aged 8–12 from all over the Liverpool, for over a year.

Together with our enthusiastic volunteers, kids work their way through fun step-by-step projects that build their programming skills.

Want to know more? Watch this video from our friends at Tech North. Or email the club host, Denise, and arrange a visit!

What’s on the menu this term?

The micro:bit! It’s a small, cheap computer, perfect for making all sorts of interactive games and projects.

Our 10 week micro:bit course introduces kids to the core concepts of programming and automation, as well as hands-on skills like electronics. It’s going to be awesome!
